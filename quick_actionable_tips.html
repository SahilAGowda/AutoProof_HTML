Quick Actionable Tips
Enable 2FA

Protect your accounts with 2FA

Overview: Enable 2FA to add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

Why it's important:

  • Prevents unauthorized access
  • Adds an extra security layer
  • Easy to set up and use

How to enable:

  1. Go to account security settings
  2. Select 'Two-Factor Authentication'
  3. Follow on-screen instructions
Use Strong Passwords

Strengthen your account security

Overview: Create unique, strong passwords for each account to enhance security.

Why it matters:

  • Prevents unauthorized access
  • Reduces risk of multiple account breaches

Password creation tips:

  • Mix letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid personal information
  • Use a password manager
Backup Data

Safeguard your information

Overview: Regular backups ensure data recovery in case of loss.

Benefits:

  • Protects against data loss
  • Enables quick data recovery

Backup steps:

  1. Choose external drive or cloud storage
  2. Set up automatic backups
  3. Verify backup integrity regularly
